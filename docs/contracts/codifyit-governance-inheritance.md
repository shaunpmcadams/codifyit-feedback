# CodifyIT Feedback Governance Inheritance Mirror

Canonical source:

- `codifyit-os/docs/contracts/codifyit-governance-inheritance.md`

## What codifyit-feedback inherits

- enterprise ownership from CodifyIT LLC
- public-intake, confidentiality, moderation, and security posture from
  `codifyit-os`
- the rule that public feedback is not the system of record for implementation
  work
- the rule that `codifyit-os` and product repos own internal governance and
  execution decisions

## What codifyit-feedback owns locally

- public discussion guidance and feedback intake language
- public-facing README, discussion posture, and moderation signals
- routing from public signal into internal product or enterprise work

## Local rule

`codifyit-feedback` may not become a public work tracker, a support desk, a
security intake channel, or a source of company-governance truth.
